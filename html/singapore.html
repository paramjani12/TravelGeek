6 days itinerary of singapore

day 1 : talking with the tall buildings of singapore

1)dukw tour : it is a vehicle that runs on both land and water. In this tour we will get to see all the modern architecture of Singapore city that includes merlion, floating platform, the pandang, old supreme court, etc.


2)garden by the bay : there are 5-6 beautiful dome with majestic flora and fauna. it also houses world’s largest indoor waterfall.


3)OCBC skyway : Awesome skywalk. It is lit up very nicely so best time is visiting it in evening time. You can walk through these beautiful tree.


4)marina bay sands : one of the world’s most expensive hotels. It houses world’s tallest infinity pool. Boat shaped hotel. You can have amazing view of the firework done at garden by the bay in evening.


5)helix bridge : very nice architecture. it is a walking bridge. You can spend 5-10 minute there to take pictures.


day 2 : rural looks of the city

1)visit Pulau Ubin : small island in Singapore. You can take historic bumboat ride to reach the island. One can enjoy cycling on the island or visit light house or walk on chek java. One can also do river kayaking.


2)Singapore flyer : enjoy the nightscape of Singapore city through these capsules like giant observatory wheel. You can have 360degree view of the city.


day 3 : it's the universal studios day

1)universal studio Singapore : Universal Studios Singapore is a theme park located within Resorts World Sentosa on Sentosa Island, Singapore. It features 28 rides, shows, and attractions in seven themed zones. enjoy your entire day at universal studio.


day 4 : chill out sentosa

1)Henderson bridge : very scene bridge. Film "Badrinath Ki Dulhania" shooting was done here.


2)sea aquarium : Largest aquarium & resort featuring 800 species of marine life in a variety of habitats.


3)Madame Tussauds : Madame Tussauds Singapore brims with amazing lifelike wax figures of big time celebrities to mingle and snap a selfie with. Stroll down the red carpet with your favorite movie stars and famous icons.


4)wings of fire : Spend an evening in Sentosa mesmerized by Wings of Time Singapore, the award-winning off-shore night show that dazzles with fountains, lasers, pyrotechnics, and majestic music.


day 5 : zoo and safari

1)Jurong bird safari : Spread across 20.2 hectares in a western corner of Singapore, this sprawling park is Asia’s largest, with a collection of over 5,000 birds from 400 species.


2)Singapore zoo : Home to over 300 species and 2,800 animals, the Singapore Zoo spans 28 hectares in the lush Mandai rainforest of Singapore.


3)river safari : The River Safari Singapore, the only river themed wildlife park in Asia takes you through the world’s most iconic rivers - from the Amazon to the Nile. Catch a glimpse of its 6,000 animal inhabitants along the way, including the famous giant pandas.


4)rainforest lumina : A twilight adventure in Southeast Asia's first multimedia night walk attraction, an interactive rainforest of mesmerizing audio-visual experiences.


5)night safari : Gear up for a journey into the jungle as the sun sets and get whole new perspective on the mysteries of the animal kingdom as you join the dark side on the world’s first after-hours animal safari.

day 6 : shopping and saying good bye with lots of osum memories

1)electronic shopping @vivocity


2)clothes and cosmetics shopping @bugis street


3)relax @changi airport lounges till you board the flight back to you home
